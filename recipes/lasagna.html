Lasagna

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's a hearty and delicious meal that is perfect for gatherings or family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef, Italian sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat. Drain any excess grease.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well and simmer for about 15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs. Mix well.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Spoon a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.
